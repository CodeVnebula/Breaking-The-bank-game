  As Henry Stickmin set out to sew the wall back together, he thought to himself,
> "How hard could it be?"
  But alas, as he put the finishing touches on his masterpiece, the wall decided
to repay the 'favor' by crashing down on him like a poorly constructed blanket fort. 
> "Note to self," Henry muttered from beneath the rubble, 
> "stick to stick figures and leave the stitching to the pros!"
